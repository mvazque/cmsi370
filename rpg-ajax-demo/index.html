Welcome to the Skyrim Character Creator. To create a new character, click the create character button. After entering in a name, race, gender, skin tone, and weight, you will be able to play around with the character's inventory.

You are also allowed to have multiple characters. Create as many as you would like, and this site will keep track of their skill stats and items.

To edit a character, you may either select the checkbox and click "Edit Character" or click directly on the character's name. It will redirect you to their skills and inventory page, with more detailed information on your current Dragonborn hero.

If one of your characters are growing old (Perhaps like "An arrow to the knee") and you are losing interest in him/her, it may be time to part ways with your once-beloved hero.
