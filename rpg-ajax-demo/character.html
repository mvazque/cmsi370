Inventory and Skills Help

Welcome to the Creator's Inventory and Skills page. To add a new item to the inventory, click the create item button. After entering in a item name, damage, weight, and value, the item will appear in your current character's inventory.

To edit an item, select the checkbox and click "Edit Item." This will allow you to change any aspect of the item in case you made a mistake!

Tired of having 10 Iron Swords? To delete an item, check the box next to their name and click delete. Don't worry, either; we will make sure your item can be deleted only after a confirmation page!

I hope you enjoy the item and skills creation suite! (And please avoid committing crimes against Skyrim and her people!)
